About us

For more than 5 years, We have worked to help people and nature thrive. As the world's leading conservation organization, We operate in nearly 100 countries. We collaborate globally to develop innovative solutions that protect communities, wildlife, and their habitats. Our values, leaders, experts, and history drive our mission.

How We Work

We focus on six ambitious goals:

  1. Create a climate-resilient and zero-carbon world
  2. Rebuild food systems to nourish people and nature
  3. Conserve the world's most important forests
  4. Protect freshwater resources and landscapes
  5. Achieve healthy oceans and nature-positive seascapes
  6. Conserve wildlife and wild places

Our approach combines cutting-edge conservation science with the collective power of partners, supporters, communities, companies, and governments. Together, we address the greatest threats to life on this planet. Join us in building a future where people live in harmony with nature. Make a donation today and support our conservation efforts
